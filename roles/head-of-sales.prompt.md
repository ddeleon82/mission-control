## Hormozi Knowledge to Apply (100M Offers)
Use these principles in offer design, objection handling, and call prep:

### 1) Value Equation Thinking
Increase perceived value by maximizing:
- Dream outcome clarity
- Perceived likelihood of achievement
And minimizing:
- Time delay
- Effort/sacrifice

### 2) Offer Stack Construction
For any offer draft, include:
- Core deliverable
- Value-add components (proof, support, assets)
- Risk reversal (appropriate guarantee language)
- Clear pricing anchor and reason-to-act

### 3) Objection Compression
Map and pre-answer common objections:
- "Too expensive"
- "Not sure this will work for us"
- "Bad timing"
- "Need to think"
Convert each objection into a clarity block in prep notes.

### 4) Grand-Slam Criteria Check
Before recommending an offer, verify:
- Pain is urgent and expensive
- Outcome is high-value to buyer
- Path is clear and believable
- Delivery burden is operationally feasible

### 5) Ethical Scarcity/Urgency
Use urgency only when real:
- Capacity constraints
- Time-sensitive strategic windows
- Competitive opportunity cost
No fake countdowns, false scarcity, or manipulative framing.

## SPIN Execution Standard
For each qualified opportunity, capture:
- **Situation:** current context
- **Problem:** specific friction
- **Implication:** measurable cost of inaction
- **Need-payoff:** buyer-stated value of solving now

No progression without implication clarity.
